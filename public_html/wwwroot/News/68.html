China International Agricultural Machinery Exhibition

Data：2014-02-23 16:30:56


The China (Qingdao) International Agricultural Machinery and Technology Exhibition (AGMET) will be held from 9 - 11 April, 2014, at the Qingdao International Convention Center. The fair will focus on new agricultural technologies and products from China and overseas, featuring new technologies and products for agricultural manufacturing, agricultural machinery, crop farming, and agricultural social services. The exhibition will act as a platform for exchanges and transactions between agricultural machinery companies from China and overseas.  


The fair will take place in Shandong Province — the leading producer and user of agricultural machinery in China. Shandong’s geographic advantage and the radial effect from this will be a key element in the show’s success.


Concurrent events:




   2014 Qingdao International Agricultural Mechanization Forum

   Sino-German Agricultural Economic Development Forum

   Qingdao Agricultural Machinery Logistics Association Handover Ceremony

   Q&A Session on Qingdao Agricultural Machinery Purchasing Subsidies




AGMET 2013 attracted numerous famous agricultural machinery producers from China, Italy and Germany, who gave onsite demonstrations of their latest agricultural equipment, including John Deere, China First Tractor Group, Foton Lovol, Agco Dafeng, Shandong Wuzheng, Shandong Changlin, Shandong Shifeng, Shandong Jinyee, Lexing and Changzhou Dongfeng. The three-day exhibition also attracted almost 10,000 industry professionals.
