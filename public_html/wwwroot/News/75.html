2015 AgrárgépShow (Agricultural Machine Show)

Data：2015-05-18 14:12:58

GROmashEXPO - AgrárgépShow (Agricultural Machine Show) presented the elite of the domestic machinery distributors, from the smallest to the market leaders. 
National machine manufacturers and distributors marched their novelties again as part of a large scale show. The exhibition which filled the entire exhibition area took place in 7 pavilions and approximately three thousand metres square in the open air.
