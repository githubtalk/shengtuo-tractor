🚜 Model-3Z Adjustable Ridge Machine – Efficient Ridging for Superior Crop Growth 🌾

The Model-3Z Adjustable Ridge Machine, designed and manufactured by Yucheng Tianming Machinery Co., Ltd, is an advanced implement used for marking ridges in the field after tillage. Built with a sturdy rectangular tube frame and angle-adjustable disc ridging units, this machine ensures precise ridging for optimal water retention and root development. The discs are crafted from high-quality 65Mn steel with heat treatment, ensuring durability and long-lasting performance.

🔹 Key Features:

✅ Sturdy & Durable Construction – Made with high-strength rectangular tube framing

✅ High-Quality 65Mn Steel Discs – Heat-treated for enhanced toughness and wear resistance
✅ Easy to Transport & Maintain – Designed for convenience and long-term use
